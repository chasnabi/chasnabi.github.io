The Tale of Chas

The first chapter of my story begins in the snowy postindustrialized Hothlike wasteland that is Buffalo, New York. A town with as much charm as it has snow where the sports teams are more depressing than the cold, exhaust-colored snow on the landscape. This young grasshopper had enough of the cold and decided to fly south. I went to college and then out of college didn't really know what I wanted to do, so I took a job that would allow me to practice circus arts in my spare time


Circus Arts are probably the best thing I've ever focused on. From the freedom of flying through the air-and the absolute trust to your partner and yourself that this entails-to eating fire without hesitation. It's a strange hobby, I know, but it's enriching and incredibly fulfilling.



Now I'm in coding bootcamp at Tech Talent South. I try to make as many sci-fi and fantasy references as I can to see if I can get our instructor, Aaron, to laugh but perhaps I'm just not trying hard enough.


That said, EVERYONE LOOK AT MY CAT!
